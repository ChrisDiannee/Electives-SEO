What is SEO?

Search engine optimization is a procedure of methodologies, techniques and strategies used to expand the measure of guests to a site by acquiring a high-positioning arrangement in the indexed lists page of a web crawler (SERP) — including Google, Bing, Yahoo and other search engines.

The difference between SEO and SEM is simply that Search Engine Optimization is part of Search engine marketing or Search marketing as it is widely known. Both processes aim in increasing visibility in search engines.
